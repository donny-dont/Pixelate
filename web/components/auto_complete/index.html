<px-auto-complete>

An auto complete form control.

Examples

An auto complete that is populated by a static list.

  • One
  • Two
  • Three
 
<px-auto-complete>
  <px-auto-complete-source>
    <ul>
      <li>One</li>
      <li>Two</li>
      <li>Three</li>
    </ul>
  </px-auto-complete-source>
</px-auto-complete>

An auto complete element that is populated from a json endpoint. Try "Polymer" or "JavaScript". 

<px-auto-complete>
  <px-auto-complete-source
    url="../../data/auto_complete.json">
  </px-auto-complete-source>
